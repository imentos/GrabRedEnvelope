## SharePlay Integration

The App uses Apple's SharePlay framework to enable multiplayer functionality over FaceTime. Here's how it works:

### What SharePlay Does
- Synchronizes game state between participants in a FaceTime call
- Transmits game events (envelope grabs, scores) peer-to-peer
- All data transmission is handled directly by Apple's GroupActivities framework

### What We Don't Have Access To
- We do NOT see or store any SharePlay session data
- We do NOT have access to your FaceTime calls
- We do NOT know who you play with or when you play
- All game data exists only during the active session and is deleted when the game ends

### Apple's Privacy
SharePlay uses end-to-end encryption through Apple's secure infrastructure. For more information about SharePlay privacy, please see [Apple's SharePlay Privacy Information](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/shareplay-iph73565d573/ios).
